CORPUSCOLETIVUSLAB, led by Brazilian artist and researcher Dra. Andressa Cantergiani, is a space for performance art practices.This lab promotes experimentation based on the creative concerns of participants and the collective. It views the body as a transdisciplinary system, exploring performance art's role in resistance and resilience. Sessions will examine performance within artistic, social, political, ecological, and technological contexts.

Open to artists and non-artists, the lab addresses the role of the body and performance in social and political spheres.
